Turning algorithms into therapies

I am a biotechnologist, biomedical engineer, and molecular biologist working to bridge the gap between what AI can imagine and what medicine truly needs.

My work is at the forefront of the AI revolution in drug discovery. I harness computational power to tackle drug-resistant infections through protein engineering‚Äîessentially teaching old proteins new tricks to fight superbugs.
