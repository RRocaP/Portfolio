Ramon Roca Pinilla

Turning algorithms into therapies

Biotechnologist and biomedical engineer focused on protein and vector engineering for antimicrobial and translational applications.

I develop and characterize recombinant proteins and viral vectors, combining computational design with wet‑lab validation. Current work spans antimicrobial protein scaffolds, inclusion bodies as functional biomaterials, and AAV vector development.
