Integrated Workflow

  1. 1

    Target Selection

    Select resistant pathogen target or viral capsid goal informed by unmet clinical need.

  2. 2

    In‑silico Design

    AI & physics-guided sequence / structure generation, filtering by stability & novelty metrics.

  3. 3

    Expression & Purification

    Produce candidates, assess solubility, folding and purity by chromatography & SDS‑PAGE.

  4. 4

    Functional Assays

    Measure antimicrobial activity or transduction efficiency; capture quantitative potency.

  5. 5

    Model Feedback

    Feed assay metrics back to design heuristics to bias next iteration toward improved efficacy.
