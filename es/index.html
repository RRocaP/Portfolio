About Me

I'm a researcher passionate about engineering biological systems to solve critical healthcare challenges.

With a background spanning molecular biology, protein engineering, and computational modeling, I work at the intersection of wet-lab experimentation and computational design. My research focuses on developing novel antimicrobial therapies and optimizing gene delivery systems for next-generation treatments.

Currently completing my PhD at the Institute for Bioengineering of Catalonia (IBEC), where I'm developing protein-based nanoparticles for targeted antimicrobial delivery and working on CRISPR-based gene therapy approaches.
